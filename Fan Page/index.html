Glass Animals

Who are the Glass Animals?

The band consists of 4 members:

  • Dave Bayley
  • Drew McFarlane
  • Edmund Irwin-Singer
  • Joe Seaward

Glass Animals are a British indie rock band formed in Oxford in 2010. Led by singer, songwriter, and producer Dave Bayley, the group also features his childhood friends Joe Seaward, Ed Irwin-Singer and Drew MacFarlane. Bayley wrote and produced all three Glass Animals albums.

Dave Bayley

Dave Algernon Bayley is the lead vocalist for Glass Animals. He was born on June 7th, 1989.

Drew McFarlane

Drew MacFarlane is the guitarist for Glass Animals. He was born on November 21st, 1988 in America

Edmund Irwin-Singer

Edmund Irwin-Singer, also known as Ed, was born on November 23rd in England. He plays the bass and keys and sometimes helps with backing vocals.

Joe Seaward

Joseph "Joe" Seaward is the drummer for the English psychedelic pop band, Glass Animals. Joe was born on October 22nd, 1988.
